Nightfall Manor: Escape from the Shadowed Realm

This game is a horror themed co-op game with pve elements where the players work together to take down enemies and complete tasks to escape. This game was made with 3 other programmers as one of our projects in Nanyang Polytechnic.

I was tasked with making the base enemy ai that can be used for all differing types of enemies, the multiplayer system with room creations, joining, leaving, as well as syncing all movement, animations and enemies in scene via Photon Pun. I also worked with the other programmers for integration of all their codes to work with multiplayer in mind as well as making the puzzle system to escape to win the game.

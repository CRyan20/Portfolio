Plooper Star Chef

For this game, I was tasked with core mechanics of the game such as the ragdoll physics of the player character, the npc interaction system (taking orders, serving and selecting their seats), the UI for the main menu and tutorial ui, the general interaction system with anything in game (doors, npcs etc), the spilling of food mechanic causing the player to ragdoll if they fall on the mess, as well as the camera system for the different minigames. I also helped my teammates with things such as the minigame system and making sure the whole game runs smoothly without bugs.

Rhythm Diner

This game is a rhythm based platformer with mechanics inspired by Crypt Of The Necrodancer made with me and an artist for our portfolios.

I was tasked with all the mechanics of the game with some that I will highlight such as a chart editor allowing for the player to make their own charts for the songs in the level to make the game harder or easier. The whole game requires the player to make actions such as taking orders, serving customers, attack, dashing etc in time with the chart that will display on the bototm of the screen.
